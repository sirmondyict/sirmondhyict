Ximending: All You Need in One There are many reasons why Ximending Shopping Center is a must-visit destination, and I’m here to tell you all about it. From delicious street food to trendy fashion, you’ll find everything you need to fill your stomach and your shopping bags. If you’re willing to walk, your grandparents will be happy to see you return with plenty of souvenirs! Ximending is packed with stores offering a diverse range of clothing styles, and the little garments you pick up are bound to become your favorite new outfits. First Step: A Taste of Ximending Of course, there are plenty of exciting places to visit, but let’s be honest—exploring on an empty stomach isn’t fun! I started my food journey with Korean cuisine, and it was amazing! One of the best things about Ximending is its diverse selection of food, ranging from Chinese and Taiwanese to Western and Indonesian flavors. But the real highlight? The street food! Ximending is famous for its Instagram-worthy snacks, like the colorful rainbow cheese toast and crispy fried milk. If those aren’t your thing, don’t worry—a warm seafood dinner will always leave you feeling satisfied. Fashion Capital of Taipei Before heading off to iconic landmarks like Chiang Kai-Shek Memorial Hall or Yehliu Geopark, why not update your wardrobe? Ximending has something for every style and occasion. Whether you prefer alternative fashion, Harajuku-style kawaii, or vintage aesthetics, you’ll find it all here. They even have quirky outfits, including Halloween clown costumes! No matter what your personal style is—even if it’s not widely accepted back home—you’ll fit right in at Ximending. And don’t forget to check out the accessories! From trendy belts to cute hairpins, the local brands here are a must-see. More Than Just Shopping Is that all you can take home? Of course not! Ximending offers more than just clothes. You can buy hats with fun slogans, perfect for a laugh, or traditional lanterns that glow like tiny moons in the night. But Ximending isn’t just about what you can buy—it’s about the experience. Once you’ve explored its lively streets, you’ll find yourself wanting to return. So, if you ever get the chance, step outside and immerse yourself in Ximending. The lanterns will welcome you back, just like an old friend.
